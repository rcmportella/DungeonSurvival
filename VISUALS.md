## Tips for Reading the Display

1. **HP Bars**: More `█` = more health, more `░` = damaged
2. **Messages**: Read bottom-up (newest at bottom)
3. **Position**: `1` always shows your location
4. **Monsters**: Single letters (A-Z) indicate type
5. **Combat**: Numbers (1-6) show each party member
6. **Map**: Explore rooms connected by corridors
7. **Symbols**: Learn the icons to navigate efficiently
